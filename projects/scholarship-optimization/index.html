How To Read This:

Choose next steps based on institutional priorities:


Maximize Access (Social Mission):

Allocate $5,000-$7,500 per high-need student.
- Serves around 400-600 highest-need students
- Meaningful aid that reduces financial barriers
- Revenue cost around 1.3% institutional impact
- Best for mission-driven, well-endowed institutions

Minimize Financial Impact:

Small scholarships ($1,000-$2,000) or no scholarships.
- Minimal revenue impact (less than 1%)
- Limited enrollment gain (10-15 additional students)
- Serves 1,500-3,000 students with small awards
- Best for revenue-constrained institutions

Strategic Middle Ground:

$3,000-$5,000 per high-need student.
- Balances access and revenue goals
- Serves 600-1,000 students meaningfully
- Around 1% revenue impact
- Best for most institutions
