# Editable financial model

Open `future_college_market_financial_model.xlsx` and change the yellow cells on the **Assumptions** sheet.

Main scenario controls:

- Projection year: 2030, 2035 or 2041
- College-going rate
- Four-year share
- Undergraduate cohort divisor
- Headcount-to-FTE factor
- Variable instructional expense share
- Institution market-share change
- State/national recruitment weights by institution scope

The **Institution Model** sheet recalculates enrollment exposure and the annual funding-gap proxy for 1,849 institutions. Finance metrics are available for 1,430 institutions.

The model is not a forecast of an institution's total operating result. It estimates only undergraduate net-tuition contribution pressure after a limited instructional-expense adjustment.
