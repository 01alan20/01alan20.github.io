# Cash-flow Reality Check panel

This update adds a configurable cash-flow panel to the Relative Value section.

## Default assumptions

- Minimum gross yield: 4.0%
- Down payment: 25%
- Interest rate: 3.5%
- Loan tenure: 25 years
- Vacancy: 1 month per year
- Fixed operating costs: S$600 per month
- Variable reserve: 5% of collected rent

The panel reports the number and share of yield-qualified project-size records that still require a monthly cash top-up, the median monthly shortfall, and the median break-even gross yield. Click a row to open the property in the full Underwriter.

Negative cash flow is not automatically a negative total return. The screen includes full mortgage payments, including principal repayment, but excludes appreciation and sale proceeds.
