Methodology

Transparent enough to challenge.

Trend universe. Apartments, condominiums and executive condominiums only. Annual summaries use medians across project-size observations in the source file. Without historical transaction counts, these are not transaction-weighted market indices.

Yield screen. Gross yield equals monthly rent × 12 ÷ purchase price. Payback is its inverse. Neither includes vacancy, tax, maintenance, financing or sale costs.

Underwriter. Stamp-duty schedules are embedded as editable model logic. Investor assumptions remain the user’s responsibility. Always verify current rules and property-specific costs before acting.

Data: URA Data Service. Stamp-duty reference: IRAS.

For research and screening only. Historical transactions do not establish present availability or future return.
