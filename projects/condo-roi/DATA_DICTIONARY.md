# Data Dictionary and Calculation Notes

## Source file: `pmi_roi_agg.csv`

One row represents a property project and area band. Historical values are aggregated observations and do not retain historical transaction counts or exact dates.

| Field | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `project` | URA project name |
| `area_bucket` | Unit floor-area band in square metres |
| `district` | Singapore postal district |
| `tenure` | Freehold or lease description |
| `propertyType` | Apartment, Condominium, Executive Condominium or landed category |
| `avg_buy_YYYY` | Average observed sale price for that project-area-year |
| `avg_rent_YYYY` | Average observed monthly rent for that project-area-year |
| `most_recent_buy` | Latest sale value retained in the aggregate source |
| `most_recent_rent` | Latest rent value retained in the aggregate source |
| `ROI` | Purchase price divided by annual rent; this is payback years, not ROI |

The recent updater may append:

| Field | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `market_segment` | CCR, RCR or OCR from URA sales data |
| `most_recent_buy_date` | Month-level date of latest recent sale |
| `most_recent_rent_date` | Month-level date of latest recent rental contract |
| `buy_count_ttm` | Recent sales count in the trailing 12-month window |
| `rent_count_ttm` | Recent rental-contract count in the trailing 12-month window |
| `avg_buy_2026` | 2026 year-to-date average sale price |
| `avg_rent_2026` | 2026 year-to-date average monthly rent |

## Derived browser dataset

`data/dashboard_data.js` is generated by `scripts/build_dashboard_data.py`.

Key derived measures:

| Measure | Formula or definition |
|---|---|
| Gross yield | Monthly rent × 12 ÷ purchase price × 100 |
| Payback | 100 ÷ gross yield |
| Price growth | Change between first and last paired annual price observations |
| Rent growth | Change between first and last paired annual rent observations |
| Yield change | Last paired annual gross yield minus first paired annual gross yield |
| District yield premium | Project-size yield minus district median project-size yield |
| Size yield premium | Project-size yield minus median yield for the same area band |
| District price discount | District median observed price minus project-size price, divided by district median |

## Evidence labels

Before recent transaction data are loaded:

- **Strong history:** at least four years with both price and rent observations.
- **Moderate history:** at least three paired years.
- **Thin history:** fewer than three paired years.
- **Incomplete:** no usable latest price-and-rent pair.

After `data/recent_snapshot.csv` exists:

- **High:** at least five trailing-year sales, ten rental contracts and both latest dates within six months.
- **Medium:** at least three sales, five rental contracts and both latest dates within twelve months.
- **Low:** thinner or older evidence.

These are project-defined screening labels, not URA classifications.

## Market trend methodology

For each selected year and filtered universe, the dashboard calculates:

- median of project-size sale-price observations;
- median of project-size rent observations;
- median of project-size gross-yield observations;
- number of available price, rent and paired observations.

The history is not transaction weighted. A project-size row with one underlying transaction can carry the same weight as a row with many transactions in the original aggregate. The recent updater improves current screening by restoring counts, but does not reconstruct historical counts.

## Underwriter formulas

### Acquisition duties

BSD uses the residential marginal bands effective from 15 February 2023:

```text
First S$180,000       1%
Next S$180,000        2%
Next S$640,000        3%
Next S$500,000        4%
Next S$1,500,000      5%
Remaining amount      6%
```

ABSD rates embedded in the buyer-profile selector are the rates effective from 27 April 2023.

### Net operating income

```text
collected rent
− maintenance
− property tax
− insurance
− repair reserve
− management fee
```

### Cash-on-cash return

```text
annual cash flow after debt service ÷ initial cash required
```

### Exit

The final modeled year includes:

```text
sale price
− outstanding loan
− selling costs
− applicable Seller's Stamp Duty
```

For residential property acquired on or after 4 July 2025, the model uses 16%, 12%, 8% and 4% SSD tiers across the first four years.

### IRR

IRR is calculated from annual equity cash flows using a numerical root search. It is sensitive to every assumption and should not be treated as a forecast.
