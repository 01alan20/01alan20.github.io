    Method and sources

    Official data, transparent calculations, and clear limits.

    The analysis uses U.S. Census Bureau American Community Survey one-year estimates for 2023 and 2024. Scores are percentile rankings among the 48 included metropolitan areas—not forecasts, causal estimates, or a definitive ranking of every place in the United States.

    Source tables: ACS B01003, B15003, B20002, B23025, B25064, and B28002. Full definitions and limitations are documented in the data dictionary.

    01

    Official inputs

    Population, earnings, gross rent, education, broadband, and employment status come from ACS detailed tables.

    02

    Reproducible build

    Run python scripts/fetch_official_data.py --refresh, then python scripts/build_dataset.py.

    03

    Interpret carefully

    Annual ACS estimates carry sampling error; the composite weights are editorial choices and can be changed in the finder.
